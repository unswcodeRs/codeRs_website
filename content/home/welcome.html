UNSW codeRs is a vibrant community fueled by students and staff who share a passion for ‘R’ programming. Our committee organises relaxed meetings perfect for mastering ‘R’ and connecting with fellow coding enthusiasts. Whether you’re a beginner venturing into coding or an experienced programmer aiming to enhance your skills, codeRs is your supportive haven.

During the third academic term of 2023 we will meet weekly every Thursday (14 Sep 2023 until 7 Dec 2023) from 10:00 am to 11:00 am.

All UNSW members are welcome, no prior experience needed, no reservations required, and snacks are on us!
